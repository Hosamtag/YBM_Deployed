#DeonteStrong

Previous Next

On the 19th of October 2019, the Your Budget Movers family endured a tragic loss. A member of our family was lost. A coworker. A friend. And most importantly, a son.

Deonte Wilkins joined our team early July of 2019. He was a great addition to our team, constantly uplifting those around him. His presence in our office and at our job sites was always complemented with laughter and his signature smile. He was always willing to help, stepping in when needed, providing guidance to those who needed it, and making sure everyone was having a good time while doing so.

Deonte Wilkins taught us to enjoy the here and now. Reminded us that no matter what, it will always be okay, to make the best out of every situation, and to always move forward. These are the traits he embodied.

Everyday we remind ourselves to live life like Deonte. To always be #DEONTESTRONG
