Breast Cancer
Awareness Campaign

Previous Next

In October of 2019, Your Budget Movers announced the first ever Breast Cancer Awareness Campaign. For every tip a customer gave to our movers after a job, Your Budget Movers matched that amount to donate to Breast Cancer Research.

Along with matching tips, we released Breast Cancer Awareness themed shirts for our staff and sold the remainder. All the proceeds were also donated to Breast Cancer Research.

Our goal is to shed light onto a condition that has affected lives and forced families apart. We know we cannot find the cure ourselves, however at Your Budget Movers we believe in being part of the solution.

Our plan is to keep this an ongoing tradition and we are excited for our campaign this upcoming October.

Our hearts are with everyone who has been affected directly or indirectly by this condition.

Stay Strong.
